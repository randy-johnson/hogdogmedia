Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Hog Dog Media ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit hogdogmedia.com or interact with our products, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run the site and respond to you:

  • Information you give us. When you fill out the contact form or sign up for the newsletter, we collect your name, email address, and any message content you provide.
  • Basic site analytics. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, timestamps) collected by our hosting provider for security and performance.
  • Cookies. We use a small number of functional cookies. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and partnership requests.
  • To send newsletter emails to subscribers (you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email).
  • To keep the site secure and fix problems.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

What we do not do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your contact-form submissions for marketing unless you separately opt in.

Service providers

We use a small set of trusted services to operate the site — including Netlify (hosting and form handling) and our email provider (newsletter delivery). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy commitments.

Data retention

Contact-form submissions are kept for as long as needed to handle your request and our records of it. Newsletter subscriber data is kept until you unsubscribe. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Email hello@hogdogmedia.com and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws like the GDPR or CCPA.

Children

Our site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be highlighted on the site.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@hogdogmedia.com or use the contact form.
